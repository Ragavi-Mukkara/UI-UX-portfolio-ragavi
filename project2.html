Case study

Movie Ticket Booking App

A mobile‑first movie ticket booking experience that simplifies discovering movies, selecting seats, and completing bookings with clarity and confidence.

Role

UI/UX Designer

Focus

Mobile UX, seat selection, transactional flow

Tools

Figma

Prototype

View Figma prototype →

Problem

Many movie ticket booking apps overwhelm users with crowded screens, promotions, and confusing layouts, especially during seat selection and payment.

This often causes frustration and anxiety on small mobile screens, where users need clarity and reassurance to complete bookings.

Design goals

  • Create a clean, distraction‑free mobile interface.
  • Simplify seat selection and booking flow.
  • Clearly communicate booking steps and pricing.
  • Ensure smooth navigation from discovery to confirmation.

User flow

  • Home → Movie Selection → Theatre & Show Time → Seat Selection → Payment → Booking Confirmation

This reflects a realistic end‑to‑end scenario for users booking tickets on mobile.

Key screens & intentions

  • Home – Helps users quickly discover currently playing movies.
  • Movie details – Provides essential information without overload.
  • Seat selection – Simplifies seat availability and selection.
  • Checkout – Clearly displays ticket count and pricing.
  • Confirmation – Reassures users with booking details.

Key screens

Key UX decisions

Mobile‑first approach

  • Designed specifically for small screens with reduced clutter.
  • Focused on essential actions and information hierarchy.

Simplified seat selection

  • Clear visual differentiation between available, selected, and unavailable seats.
  • Reduced confusion during high‑decision moments.

Clear progress awareness

  • Logical, step‑by‑step booking flow.
  • Users always know where they are in the process.

UX improvements

Aspect Improvement
Navigation Clear and linear booking flow.
Seat selection Improved visibility and interaction design.
Information clarity Essential details surfaced at the right time.
User confidence Confirmation screen reduces anxiety and uncertainty.

Impact & takeaways

This project showed how small UX decisions significantly impact user confidence during transactional experiences.

It helped me practice balancing visual simplicity with functional clarity in mobile applications.
